Warehousing & Fulfillment

Offshore Global Logistics continuously strives at being recognized as the best logistics and courier service provider in Kenya. We achieve this by maintaining a high quality service every time. Our service gives you the power and flexibility to effectively manage all your shipments through our state of the art online tracking system.

Our modernized warehousing & distribution facility located off Mombasa Road in Nairobi: a very close proximity to the both the ICDN (Inland Container Depot Nairobi) and JKIA, is well equipped to meet our client’s cargo handling needs. The technological innovations we have employed in our facility i.e the use of a Warehousing Management System, Smart Scanners among other technologies, has boosted our efficiency in stock management and control.

Supported by our well experienced and capable personnel, exceptional in managing end-to-end inventory planning, we offer replenished tailor cut warehousing service and proficiency to handle a wide assortment of products to meet our clients’ needs. Our clients reap from shared cost benefits ranging from shared space, labor, equipment, and technology. Our Warehouse management system (WMS) offers our clients with 100 degrees visibility of their stock among other capabilities that include;

  • 24Hr web-based Access through multiple options including mobile device
  • Ability to place orders for dispatch. With multi-level approvals- if needed
  • Access to real time stock reports
  • Access to real time data on both receipts and dispatches
  • Ability to get re-order level alerts
  • Ability to set authorized consignees of cargo
  • Ability to authorize SKU conversions & transfers to damaged/unusable stock
  • System based stock take
  • System integration options
  • Repackaging, Strapping, shrink-wrapping & Palletizing for shipping
  • Bonded warehouse
  • Barcodes, KEBS, KRA, Warranty sticker
  • Kitting & Light Assembly Services
  • Product Rework & Tech Support Services
  • Inspection services- SGS, Bureau Veritas, CCIC
  • Stickering Services e.g. Labelling, Serializing
